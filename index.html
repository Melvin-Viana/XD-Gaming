About Us

At 2001 Soren Bjerg founded XD Gaming Cafe to share his love with gamers. He built this community to give gamers a place to enjoy and play alongside gamers. Serving pc and console gamers alike. XDG also is an eSports team in Overwatch, League of Legends, and Counter Strike.

XDG has plans to plant 10 gaming cafes in 2019 throughout the world. The XDG eSports team hopes to branch out into Apex Legends and Fortnite.
